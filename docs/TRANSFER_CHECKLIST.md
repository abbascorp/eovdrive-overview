# Transfer Checklist

## Before payment

- Buyer reviews overview repository
- Seller provides demo video or screenshots if requested
- Buyer reviews test summary and due diligence notes
- Both sides agree on final price and transfer method

## Payment method

Recommended:

- Escrow service
- Platform-protected payment
- Milestone-based payment

Avoid sending the full private source package before payment protection is in place.

## Delivery after payment/escrow

Seller provides:

- Full sale-ready ZIP
- Private source repository access, if applicable
- Documentation and validation materials
- Sample `.eov` volumes
- Handoff notes

## Inspection

Suggested inspection period for a low-ticket source-code asset:

- 24 hours for simple transfer
- 48 hours if the buyer wants to build and test locally

## After approval

- Buyer confirms receipt
- Escrow/payment is released
- Repository ownership or archive delivery is finalized
- Seller removes buyer-specific temporary access links if any
